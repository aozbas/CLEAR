CLEAR

skin-lesion identification

CLEAR classifies skin-lesion photos into broad categories using a small neural network. It runs on your phone, sends each scan to a private backend for prediction, and stores your scan history securely.

It does not diagnose disease. It cannot replace a dermatologist. Predictions are probabilistic and routinely wrong on edge cases.
